Welcome To Coding4Kidz!

Who are we?

  1. We are students at Del Norte High School with a passion for computer science and helping others.
  2. When we were in elementary/middle school, we experienced programming classes for the first time and enjoyed it thoroughly
  3. As we got into high school, this motivated us to create a free resource that all children in our community can access.
  4. That is how Coding4Kidz was born.
This website was created by AP Computer Science A students -- Arnav Palkhiwala, Aman Jain, Christopher Duroiu, and Raquib Alam -- in Spring of 2022.
